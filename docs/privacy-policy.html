Privacy Policy

Remote for Google TV
Last updated: July 4, 2026

Summary

This app does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Everything needed to control your TV stays on your iPhone and your local home network.

What the app does

Remote for Google TV connects to a Google TV or Android TV on the same Wi‑Fi network to send remote commands and launch apps. Pairing uses a code shown on your TV.

Data we collect

None. We do not operate servers and do not receive your data.

Data stored on your device

The app may store locally on your iPhone:

  • Your TV’s name and IP address
  • Whether pairing completed
  • TLS client certificates used to communicate with your TV

This information is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Local network

The app uses your local network to discover and control your TV. iOS will ask for permission before local network access is allowed.

Third parties

This app is not affiliated with Google LLC. Streaming apps you launch are governed by their own privacy policies.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
